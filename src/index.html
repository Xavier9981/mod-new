WELCOME TO THE OFFICIAL WEBSITE OF THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE

The Ministry of Defence is charged with the critical responsibility of preserving, protecting and defending the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Zambia for the sole purpose of ensuring the country, its citizens and residents are safeguarded from both internal and external aggression.
